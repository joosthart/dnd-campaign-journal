## Wild Beyond the Witchlight

Four strangers, each carrying the weight of something lost, converged on the Witchlight Carnival — a place of impossible wonder that arrives once every eight years. What began as an evening of cupcakes, swan rides, and balloon animals unraveled into a conspiracy of disappearances, stolen voices, and mirrors that lead to other worlds.

Now they walk the misty paths of Prismeer, a splintered fey realm ruled by a coven of hags, searching for what was taken from them — and for the dormant queen who might set it all right.
