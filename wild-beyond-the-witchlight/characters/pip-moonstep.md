**Race:** Haregon | **Class:** Bladesinger Wizard | **Age:** 22

A rabbit-folk with light brown skin, darker hair, and pale blue eyes. Fights with a dance-like bladesinging style. Carries a short sword.

Eight years ago, Pip visited the Witchlight Carnival and lost his rhythm — the magical feeling that guided his bladesinging. He returned to find answers. At the Big Top, he tried to dance and his lost rhythm betrayed him. The crowd went quiet.

He was the one who convinced Baba to step through the mirror, gently taking her hand at the threshold. In Prismeer, he feels a persistent tingling at the base of his skull — something in this realm calls to him.

Sir Talavar spoke of a fae named **Vorneal Stellhour** — someone proficient in the arcane who fought with swords, from Prismeer — who may be connected to Pip's past.

In combat, Pip activates Bladesong (AC 19), summons a Shadow Blade, and dual-wields psychic energy and his rapier.
