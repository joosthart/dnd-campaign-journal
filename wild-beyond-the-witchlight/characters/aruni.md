**Race:** Elf | **Class:** Druid | **Age:** 142

An elderly druid with light dreadlocks and a well-used walking staff. Carries a large backpack bursting with plants and potions. Prefers nature to cities.

Aruni is searching for someone but doesn't know who — only that small signs keep pointing her forward. Her birds led her to Erophin, and through him, to the carnival and beyond.

Warm and occasionally secretive — she hid her invisibility cupcake from the group until the perfect moment ("Why wouldn't you tell us in the first place?"). Won a cupcake eating contest with druidic constitution and a natural 20 deception to keep the prize secret.

In combat, Aruni's Moonbeam proved devastating against the constrictor snakes. Her foraging skills (natural 20 with Erophin) earned the group truffles that won over Jingle Jangle.
