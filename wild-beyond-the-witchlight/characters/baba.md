**Race:** Tabaxi | **Class:** Monk | **Age:** ~9

A tabaxi with purple skin and white hair. Worked at the Witchlight Carnival doing odd jobs — lifting things, performing acrobatics — in exchange for housing. Doesn't quite know how long she'd been there. Received a free ticket from an anonymous benefactor.

Sharp-tongued and unimpressed by most things, Baba was the most reluctant to step through the mirror. What she lost remains unclear — it may be her memories or her grip on time itself. As she put it: "I don't know if I want to do it with you guys." But she went anyway.

She pulled off the heist of the campaign — snipping Mr. Witch's pocket watch chain while invisible, with a natural 20 on sleight of hand. At the Big Top, her flaming hoop leap brought the house down.

In combat, Baba uses Step of the Wind, Flurry of Blows, and Deflect Attack — completely negating a 13-damage constriction in the snake fight.
