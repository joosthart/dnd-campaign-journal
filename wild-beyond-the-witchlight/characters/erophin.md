**Race:** Wood Elf | **Class:** Swarmkeeper Ranger | **Age:** 150

A wood elf with golden eyes, green-splashed skin, and two large horns. Dresses in worn black and brown leather. Carries an elegant black sword with an emerald in the hilt and a longbow. Birds constantly flock around him.

His birds led him to the Witchlight Carnival with unusual urgency. He doesn't know what he lost — only that something is missing. His quiet kindness throughout the carnival — giving a button to a monkey, rescuing a halfling, spreading cheer without credit — earned him the title of **Witchlight Monarch** and the Charm of the Monarch: butterfly wings and flight, usable three times (one spent).

In combat, Erophin uses Hunter's Mark and his swarm of birds to harry enemies. His birds finished a giant snake in spectacular fashion — swooping down and turning its corpse to stone.

Against Agdon Longscarf on the Tollway, Erophin popped his butterfly wings a second time and conjured Zephyr Strike for a speed boost, circling overhead with his longbow. It was his Ensnaring Strike — a single arrow that bloomed vines around the invisible, sprinting brigand — that finally brought the Brigand Prince face-down in the mud.
