# D&D Campaign Journal

A collection of D&D campaign journals, powered by [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code) for session summaries and narrative tracking.

## Campaigns

### [Wild Beyond the Witchlight](wild-beyond-the-witchlight/)

A D&D 5e adventure through the Feywild. Four adventurers navigate a mysterious carnival and the lands beyond.

**The Party:**
- **Erophin** — Wood elf ranger with golden eyes, horns, and a flock of birds
- **Pip Moonstep** — Haregon bladesinger searching for his lost rhythm
- **Baba (Uma)** — Tabaxi monk with purple skin who works at the carnival
- **Aruni** — 142-year-old druid searching for someone she can't remember

## How It Works

Session transcripts are processed with Claude Code to produce narrative summaries. Each campaign folder contains its own `CLAUDE.md` with workflow details.
